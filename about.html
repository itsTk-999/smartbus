About Lil Tk Travellers

Connecting Kenya with safe, comfortable, and affordable bus travel.

Who We Are

At Lil Tk Travellers, we are committed to revolutionizing the way you travel across Kenya. Our goal is to provide seamless booking experiences, reliable buses, and a safe journey for every passenger. With modern technology and a dedicated team, we bring you the most convenient travel solution at your fingertips.


Whether you're commuting for work, visiting family, or exploring new destinations, Lil Tk Travellers is here to make your journey memorable and hassle-free.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create a connected network of bus services that empower travelers to book easily, travel safely, and reach their destinations on time.

Our vision is to be the leading bus booking platform in East Africa, recognized for innovation, trust, and world-class customer service.
